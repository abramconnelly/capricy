About

This is a free/libre/open source mood tracking application.

You can use this application to track your mood. Each entry can have notes attached or pictographic indicators of what you did that day or event.

With enough entries, you have the potential to see trends and analize what else might have caused your mood to change.

Data is kept completely on your local machine. You can export your data to a SQLite database any time through the config menu. You can import your data as well.

Source code available under an AGPLv3 license.
